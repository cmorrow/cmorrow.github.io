Cox Communications

As the third-largest cable provider in the nation, Cox Communications Inc. is noted for its high-capacity, reliable broadband delivery network and superior customer care. Cox has more than 6 million total residential and commercial customer relationships, over 22,000 employees and a firm commitment
to education.
www.cox.com

National Association for Sport and Physical Education

The preeminent national authority on physical education and a recognized leader in sport and physical activity, the National Association for Sport and Physical Education (NASPE) is a non-profit, professional membership association that sets the standard for practice in physical education and sport.
www.naspeinfo.org

The President's Challenge

The President’s Challenge is a long-standing program of the President’s Council on Physical Fitness and Sports which recognizes individuals ages 6 and above for getting and staying more fit and active. Americans of all abilities are encouraged to improve their physical activity/fitness status; track their minutes and, earn presidential recognition for their efforts. www.presidentschallenge.org

Action for Healthy Kids

Action for Healthy Kids® , a public-private partnership of 60 organizations and government agencies, is a national nonprofit organization that addresses the epidemic of overweight, undernourished and sedentary youth by focusing on improving nutrition and physical activity policies and practices
in schools.
www.ActionForHealthyKids.org
