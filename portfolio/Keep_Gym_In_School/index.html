Our Mission

NFL Network believes that every student should have the opportunity to participate in quality physical education. We’ve created Keep Gym in School to increase access to in-school physical activity and to teach skills needed to establish and sustain healthy lifestyles.

Community Investment

Through Keep Gym in School, NFL Network is providing grants directly to schools across country to invest in our children’s physical education and our country’s future. The grants are being used to build new tracks and playing fields, refurbish gyms and indoor PE facilities, purchase new equipment and supplement teacher salaries. We’re helping more kids have access to more hours of PE every week.

Keep Gym in School President's Challenge

Keep Gym in School is incorporating the President’s Challenge, a physical activity and fitness recognition program of the President’s Council on Physical Fitness and Sports and creating the "Keep Gym in School President’s Challenge" program. No matter what your activity and fitness level, the Keep Gym in School President’s Challenge can help motivate you to improve.
