Breaking the Ice.

 

First contact with boarding schools can be made directly from our site through the "Say Hello" tool. Follow-up contact is typically made through the boarding school's Admission office. You may request materials such as viewbooks, videos, and detailed curriculum information, or you may be at the point where you'd like to schedule a visit. Either way, the Admission office is the way to go.
