Overview

Boarding school admission officers need a specialized set of skills. This academy is designed to teach those important skills to individuals with less than two years of experience. A dynamic team of proven admission leaders guides participants through a comprehensive workshop agenda, offering pragmatic solutions to the challenges faced by those that present the mission of the school to the general public. Truly a nuts-and-bolts workshop, the techniques and methods presented will benefit your students and campus community. This Academy has me so excited to be working in an admission office. I cant wait to get back to my school and start! Admission Academy attendee
